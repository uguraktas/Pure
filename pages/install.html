Install for Rails 3.1+

In your Gemfile: 

						gem 'pure-sass'

Then run: 

							$ bundle install

Restart your server. Then rename application.css to application.scss: 

 
							mv app/assets/stylesheets/application.css app/assets/stylesheets/application.scss

Delete the sprocket directive in application.scss: 

							*= require_tree .

Import Pure-Sass at the beginning of application.scss. All additional stylesheets must be imported below Bourbon: 

 
							 @import "Pure";
